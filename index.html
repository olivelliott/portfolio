About Me

I have lived in the beautiful mountain range of Appalachia for 7 years now in a small mountain town named Boone in North Carolina. The beautiful summers keep me energized through the cold and snowy winters.


I am a 2019 honors graduate of Appalachian State University with a degree in Cultural Anthropology and a focus on women's alternative economies and sustainable practice. This lends an interesting perspective to my work as I seek to create accessible development projects that can provide information and designs across the world.
